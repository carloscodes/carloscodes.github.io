About

Hello, my name is Carlos Chavarria. I've been a web developer for about a year now. As I am emerging into the programming world I am simultaneously completing my Computer Science degree at the University of California, Irvine. My goal in doing this is to help people get beautiful projects at a reasonable price. If you have an idea, I can make it happen. Difficult projects and challenges are why I do this.


I am a front-end and back-end web developer, providing creative solutions.

Services

Front End Development

Arguably the most important aspect of the website, the presentation of the site and ease of use is important in engaging customers with content. No one wants to do business with someone who has a hideous website. My goal is to create beautiful sites with a design meant to engage the visitor.

Back End Development

Besides having a website look good, you also need it to perform without a hiccup. Back End development is where I thrive, I implement any function to the site you may need and do so in the most efficient way, saving you money, and your potential clients, time. With a security first foundation, you can rest easy that your site will function as it is meant to while protecting you and your client's information.

Maintenance

I offer several maintenance plans depending on the needs and condition of the site. Spanning a wide array of options including but not limited to: plug-ins, updating blog posts, or even running the entire site. For pricing please feel free to contact me, prices vary.

My Skill

The difference between a great programmer and a good programmer is about 10:1. In most professions the difference between someone great and someone good is negligible, a lot of the times you will often receive the same product with either choice. On your journey to find someone to build the project you envision, you may come across some people who can complete your job for an extremely low price, but the quality won't be that great. As I am a student first and have a lot of knowledge and ability, I try to bridge that gap between cheap and quality, in return for experience.
